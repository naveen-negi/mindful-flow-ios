Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Privacy First: Pranayama Tracker does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your practice is completely private.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Practice sessions (date, duration, breathing ratios)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Progress statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Data Storage

All information is stored locally using:

  • iOS Local Storage (for app settings)
  • Device-only storage (no cloud sync)

If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed from your device.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Permissions

The app may request:

  • Haptic Feedback: To provide tactile cues during breathing phases (optional)

No other permissions are required or requested.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at: support@pranayamatracker.app
